# Penalty Kings — Store Image Prompts (icon + thumbnails)

AI image-generation prompts for the Roblox game page. Works with Midjourney, DALL·E, Leonardo,
Ideogram, etc.

**Specs**
- **Icon:** 1:1, **512×512** (the small square shown in search/sort).
- **Thumbnails:** 16:9, **1920×1080**, up to 10 (the big images on the game page).

**Rules of thumb**
- AI renders **text badly** — generate the *art only*, then add titles/labels yourself in Canva /
  Photoshop. (Every prompt below leaves clean space for that.)
- Keep ONE consistent style across all images so the page looks cohesive: *Roblox-style 3D render,
  blocky characters, glossy, vibrant saturated colors, dramatic stadium lighting.*
- For Midjourney add `--ar 1:1` (icon) or `--ar 16:9` (thumbs) and `--style raw --v 6`.
- Ideogram/DALL·E are better if you DO want baked-in text.

## After you generate

- Add the **title + 1 short benefit line** per image in Canva (big bold font, high contrast,
  drop shadow). Keep the wording the same family: "Would You Rather… but it's REAL", "Become ANY
  Legend", "World Cup 2026".
- **Icon = your highest-CTR asset.** Make 3–4 icon variants and A/B test which gets more plays.
- Reuse these same renders as TikTok/Shorts cover frames + your channel banner for a consistent
  brand.
- Roblox upload: Creator Dashboard → your experience → Thumbnails (icon + up to 10 images). Lead
  with thumbnail #1 (the hero runner shot).
