**Game:** Penalty Kings: World Cup Shootout (Roblox)
**Hook:** "Would You Rather… but it actually transforms you" + real World Cup 2026 hype.
**Goal of short-form:** drive Roblox searches for **"Penalty Kings"** → plays → favorites.

## 0. One-time setup (do first)

- **Accounts:** TikTok + YouTube (Shorts) + Instagram Reels. Same @handle everywhere. Name them
  something searchable: `@PenaltyKingsRBLX` or similar.
- **Bio:** "⚽ Would You Rather… but it's REAL | Play free on Roblox 👇" + link (Roblox game URL
  or a Linktree).
- **Pinned content:** pin your best transformation clip on each profile.
- **Tools:** screen-record in Studio/app (vertical 1080×1920), CapCut for cuts + captions.
- **Always:** on-screen text the whole clip (most watch muted), game name visible, end on a
  question. The in-game ⚽ PENALTY KINGS watermark now shows during every Choices run + on the
  result card — so even other people's recordings carry the name.

## Hashtag sets (rotate, ~5 per post)

- Core: `#roblox #robloxgames #fyp #wouldyourather #worldcup2026`
- Swap-ins: `#robloxedit #footballtiktok #soccer #messi #ronaldo #worldcup #robloxviral`
- Always include your branded tag: `#PenaltyKings`

## The format (every single clip)

1. **0–2s hook** = the payoff or the question FIRST (never the menu/loading).
2. **2–6s** = choice → transformation (the surprise).
3. **End** = on-screen question + "search Penalty Kings on Roblox". Loop-friendly.
